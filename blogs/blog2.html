Why I aspire into becoming a web developer?

I will be penning down why I want to get into web dev. So let's start with what I did before BBA. I am an engineering drop-out. Honestly, we all know that you don't have to read theory or get a certain degree to get started with development. My journey has been similar. Although I hated engineering subjects I am thoroughly enjoying the process of building the web lately. Yeah you'd wonder why am I realising this now? I recently stumbled upon Tanay's channel and got started with level 0 and I also know that the recent neog grads, many of whom are not even from an engineering background and landed their first tech job. This is pretty much why I am convinced that neogcamp could be a perfect platform where folks irrespective of their background are welcome to get started with their full stack web development journey. I could not help but get started with level 0 and wish to have a similar experience with web development. I've started understanding HTML, CSS, Javascript, ReactJS and Git. I am still exloring these and enjoying the process. So I guess that's it for now. Stay tuned for more.
